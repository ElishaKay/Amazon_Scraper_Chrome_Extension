Gather My Purchase History

Please note:

a) My Amazon History will never save sensitive data like credit cards or order ids - instead, the app only fetches whatever is necessary to populate your Social Shopping Dashboard, such as Public Product Details.

b) Once the fetching is complete, you will have the option to hide, show, or write reviews on any items of your Amazon Purchase History.
